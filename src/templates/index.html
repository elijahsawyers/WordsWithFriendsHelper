Helper!

Instructions

1. First, fill out the game board by clicking on a cell and typing the key to place into the selected cell.

2. Next, input your current seven letters into the cells on the right-hand side of the screen.

3. Lastly, click "Go", and the best possible move will be displayed on the gameboard. To keep it in the board, click "keep". To remove it, click "Discard".

To deselect a character, re-click it or press escape.

To delete a character, click it, and press delete (backspace).

To clear the board, click "clear".

Note: wildcard characters aren't yet supported.
